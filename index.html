About Me

I recently completed my 12th standard in 2025 and am currently studying at Freshworks STS Software Academy with the goal of building a strong career in the IT industry.

My journey into web development started during my 11th standard, where I built my first full-stack project by learning from online tutorials.

Since then, I’ve been consistently improving my skills by building real projects rather than just learning theory.

I have experience developing functional full-stack applications using Django and PostgreSQL, and I am currently expanding my backend knowledge by learning FastAPI. I enjoy understanding how systems work end-to-end—from database design to backend logic and frontend user experience.
